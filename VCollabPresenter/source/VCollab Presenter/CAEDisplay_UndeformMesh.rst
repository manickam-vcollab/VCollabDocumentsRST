CAE Undeformed Mesh
===================

The **Undeformed Mesh** option displays or hides undeformed mesh in
addition to original mesh.

Undeformed mesh is displayed in different modes

-  Wire frame.

-  Transparent

-  Feature Edges

-  Transparent with Edges
