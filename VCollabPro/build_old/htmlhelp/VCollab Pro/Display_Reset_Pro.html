Reset

Reset option found in the display menu is used to reset a scene to a predefined standard front view In VCollab Pro, users can:

  • Load a model and observe the view
  • Transform the model
  • Explore the model
  • Pick and drop a part

After doing any of the above, click Display | Reset or Ctrl+R to reassemble all geometries and display front view.
