Zoom Region

VCollab Pro users can use the Zoom Region option to magnify and view inner details of a model.

Steps to use Zoom Region

  • Click Tools | Zoom Region to enable.
  • Move the mouse to the region.
  • Left click and hold.
  • Drag the mouse and notice that a rectangle is drawn.
  • Drag further until the region to be zoomed is covered by the rectangle.

  • Release the mouse button up and notice that the region is zoomed with animation.

  • Repeat the above steps for magnifying further.
